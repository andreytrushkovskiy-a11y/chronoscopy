02 — SYNOPSIS

One life,
or the world.

Mark Hopkins, a brilliant theoretical physicist, loses his wife Lily in a car accident. Consumed by grief, he makes an impossible choice: build the Chronoscope — a machine that can rewrite the past.

With engineer Oleksandr and backed by the ambitious financier Eric, Mark travels back to save her. But every attempt tears the fabric of reality — Spatial Deformations spreading across the planet. As the world teeters on collapse, Mark faces an unbearable question.

Is one life worth the existence of billions?
